About Us

Founded in 2015 by a team of certified sewing machine technicians, SewingManuals.xyz began with a simple mission: to make sewing machine repair accessible to everyone. Our founders spent decades working in repair shops and noticed that many issues could be easily fixed at home with the right guidance.

Today, we're proud to be the largest online resource for sewing machine repair, serving over 100,000 users monthly. Our core values of accessibility, accuracy, and user empowerment drive everything we do. We believe that with the right information and support, anyone can maintain and repair their beloved sewing machine.
